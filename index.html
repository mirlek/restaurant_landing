About Restaurant

The gastrobar presenets the local fusion of European techniques with Asian notes. Bright raw bar, based on rethinking the classic recipes of raw fish and meat dishes with an Asian sound. The dishes are made of Russian products, in order to show the potential for using Russian raw materials.

"This is not a trivial bar, where they come only for a drink. This is a multidisciplinary space, whose diverse cuisine can surprise even the most fastidious gourmets. In a gastropub, as nowhere else, it is easy to choose dishes for your preferences."
